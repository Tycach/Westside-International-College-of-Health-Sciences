Westside International College of Health Sciences (WICHS)

Bridging Knowledge and Practice for Global Impact

Who We Are

Westside International College of Health Sciences (WICHS) is a leading institution focused on health sciences and management education. It aims to connect theoretical knowledge with practical skills to develop professionals who can impact global healthcare. WICHS emphasizes innovation, research, and holistic education for students' growth. The university collaborates closely with Westside Family University Hospitals to provide integrated learning experiences.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a global leader in shaping the future of health sciences and research, producing professionals who excel in their fields and contribute to the betterment of society.

Mission

To provide world-class education and training in health sciences by fostering innovation, research, and ethical practices while addressing the dynamic needs of the global community.

Academic Programmes

Diploma Programmes

Programme Duration Qualifications
Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology 3 years Mean Grade C, C in English or Kiswahili and Physics/Physical Sciences or Chemistry C- in Biology/Biological Sciences and in any one of the following: Metal Work, Physics, Physical Science, Chemistry, Mathematics
Diploma in Community Health 3 years KCSE Mean Grade of “C” (Plain). Mandatory subjects: “C-”(Minus) in English or Kiswahili “D+” (Plus) in Biology or Biological Sciences. A minimum of “D” (plain) in any one of the following: Mathematics, Chemistry, Home Science, Agriculture, Physics or Physical Science.
Diploma in Community Oral Health 3 years Mean Grade C, C in English or Kiswahili and Biology/Biological Sciences C- in any two of the following: Physics/Physical Sciences, Chemistry, Mathematics
